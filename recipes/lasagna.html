Lasagna for Garfield

Description

In 1852, my great great great grandmother Paulineila went to a local village gathering to celebrate St. Frankenstein's Day and his contributions to the universe as we know it. Upon arrival, she was lifted off her feet by the scent of something she had never heard of, let alone seen or smelled. Gooey cheese mixed with ground meat, layered between sheets of handmade pasta to create a sandwhich cake known regionally as "Lesinguinea", known today as Smorgastorta. Next to it was someone serving lasagna, which was also good.

Fast forward 172 years, with the help of today's premiere archaeologists, we're now able to create the only official authentic version of lasagna as tasted in 1852, and loved by renowned 1980s rebel cat, Garfield. With only 173 ingredients, it's as easy as ever to throw together this recipe from ingredients you already have in your walk-in pantry and 2nd basement freezer.
